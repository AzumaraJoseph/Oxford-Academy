Our Guest Speakers

Azumara, Joseph Chukwuemeka

Zoologist and a Programmer

Azumara, Joseph Chukwuemeka is a Nigerian and certified Zoologist. He is venturing into modern technology, which is the fast growing and lucreative aspect of this evolving planet. He is enthusiastic and passionate about what he does in all spheres of life.

David, Olusoga

Historian, broadcaster and film-maker

David Olusoga is a British_Nigerian historian, broadcaster and filmmaker who has made TV series including Empire and the BAFTA winning show Britain's Forgotten Slave Owners. His award-winning book Black & British: A Forgotten History was also made into a major BBC TV series.

Eliza, Manningham-Buller

Former Director General of MI5

Eliza, Manningham-Buller is the former Director General of MI5, the British Intelligence Agency. During her career she dealt with the aftermath 0f 9/11 and the threat of terrorism, and later became chair of the Welcome Trust, one of the world's largest charitale organizations for medical research.

Shami, Chakrabarti

Politcian and Activist

Baroness Shami Chakrabarti is a lawyer an a member of the British House of Lords who served as director of the human rights group liberty., campaigning against "excessive" anti-terror lgislation. A frequent public speaker, she has been described as "the most effective public affairs lobbyist of the past 20 years".
